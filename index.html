    Cybersecurity services adapted to your business needs

    Our services are informed by extensive experience providing advice to security teams of different sizes, discovering a wide range of vulnerabilities, managing bug bounty programs and meaningful solutions for advanced problems

    Our Services

    We provide multiple cybersecurity services adapted to your needs
    Web security audit
    Our team performs top tier web security audits following the OWASP methodology to analyze and evaluate your site weaknesses.
    Mobile Security Audit
    We conduct security audit and testing on your mobile applications to detect any potential vulnerabilities that could affect your users or infrastructure.
    Source Code Audit
    Source code reviews are an important part for securing your SDLC. We will manually review your code in order to detect any potential vulnerabilities and design software securely.
    Smart Contract Security Audit
    Our team manually reviews the source code and design of your smart contracts to identify vulnerabilities, analyzes their impact, and provides advice to improve the security of your product.
    Managed Bug Bounty Program
    With over 6 years of experience managing and participating in bug bounty programs, our team will help set up your bug bounty program and ensure it becomes a success.
